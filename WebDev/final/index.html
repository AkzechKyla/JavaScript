About CCIS Concern Hub

CCIS Concern Hub is a student-focused platform that allows users to securely submit their academic concerns, complaints, and requests to the appropriate CCIS administrators. Designed to promote accountability, communication, and fast resolution of issues, this tool ensures that every voice is heard.

Benefits of Smarter Concern Management

Progress
Transparency

Provides real-time updates on concerns, ensuring students stay informed about the status and resolution progress.

Concerns
Organization

Streamlines the classification and tracking of student concerns, allowing for efficient prioritization and resolution.

Management
Efficiency

Optimizes the concern-handling process, reducing delays and improving response times for better student support.

CCIS Concern Hub

is a web-based application and a student concern management system dedicated to the College of Computer and Information Sciences (CCIS) at PUP-Manila.
